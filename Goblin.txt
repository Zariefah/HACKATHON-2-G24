To become a great player, you must successfully complete each level. Every player will face a crucial test to demonstrate their mettle in order to complete Level 1 of their training, which involves navigating the maze-like corridors inhabited by a lone goblin named Snik. As a result, you must battle with the goblin in the perilous depths of an ancient dungeon. In light of this sobering fact, you must think fast whether to engage the goblin in combat, use skills, or retreat from the dangerous situation.
