As the player emerges victorious from their intense battle with the horde of zombies, a deafening roar echoes through the dungeon, signifying a new challenge. The ground trembles beneath their feet as a colossal white dragon awakens from its slumber, drawn by the chaos caused by the undead. With fiery breath and icy claws, the dragon poses a formidable threat, testing the soldier's mettle and pushing their skills to the limit. The player must summon every ounce of courage and resourcefulness to confront this majestic yet deadly adversary, knowing that the consequence of the zombie encounter has unleashed a far greater danger upon their path to becoming a legendary warrior.
